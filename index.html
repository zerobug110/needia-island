about needia

Needia is a mandinka world means pleasure. Needia is an island located in west africa with a very beautiful hotel and a rich culture. The land area of needia is 12 kilo meters square full of green forest with over four thousand different bird species. Needia has everything a person can dream of from beautiful beaches to amazing hotel with nice outdoor and indoors pool. Tourist and both citizens can very and stay there holiday, vacation weddings etc. Needia is a holiday destination for many people that want to relax and just forget about the problems of the world. We have been in the business for 25 years now and all we do is satisfy our client and make sure they have the best time ouf their lives with us.

bicycle

Visitor can take a ride across the island. we have more than 300 bicycle tracks, that are very smooth and has a map of the island in every 1 kilo meters. There are more than one thousand bicycle available, for both adult and kids. we organize monthly bicycle race events for our guest. Bicycle tour guid is only available for vip visitors. We also have emergency bicycles parked in the woods for any visitor that happen to damage while riding, every bicycle has a first aid box attach to with our emergency phone.
